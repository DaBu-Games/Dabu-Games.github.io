About me

Hello, and thank you for taking an interest in my portfolio. My name is Daan Buitelaar, and I’m a second year game developer at HKU. I’ve loved games ever since I played the first Skylanders as a kid. It showed me what it feels like to step into a fantasy world and be the main character, instead of just watching someone else’s story. It made me realize that games can tell stories in ways no other medium can.

My goal is to one day start my own company with two other partners and create games that go beyond just entertainment. I aim to use the incredible storytelling power of games to show people that games can have a positive impact.
